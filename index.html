Perforce

Point and click puzzle game where you approve and deny parcels being sent by aliens.

First worked on as a university project, it was later massively improved during DARE Academy.

DARE Academy is the premier game development competion hosted by Abertay University, 6 teams make the final after an application and interview round.

Following four weeks of hot housing and an open play event, an ultimate winner is decided upon by a panel of industry judges.

For this project I worked as one of two programmers, alongside artists, desginers, an audio designer, and a producer.

The game was made in Unreal Engine 5 and as a programmer I worked on many differnt parts of the design, from UI to scoring mechanics, improving my skills with each new feature.

    Features I worked on during professional project module

  • Parcel generation
  • Stamping
  • Journal system
  • Save system
  • Scoring system

    Features I worked on during DARE Academy

  • Multiple day system
  • Parcel generation rework
  • Fragile taping
  • Reworked item database
  • Daily bans and rules
  • Scoring system rework
  • Tutorial popups
