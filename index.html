Bullet Types and Status Effects Mechanic

Unreal Engine 5 code project where different bullets apply different effects to objects in the scene

Game mechanic made in Unreal Engine 5 as part of third-year university coursework.

The project was fully made using C++ with minimal blueprint useage for UI.

The mechanic uses a status effects component to apply different effects to placeholder enemies based on the bullet type fired:

  • Set enemies on fire dealing damage over time, the fire spreads to other burnable enemies
  • Turn enemies into the centre of a "gravity field", pulling other objects towards them
  • Turn an enemy into a "stacking bomb" that explodes after a time and is stronger the more times it is applied
When taking damage the placeholder enemies will flash and display the damage recently taken.

Bullet Hell Infinite

Infinite runner bullet hell made using SFML

Made as part of first year university coursework using SFML, Bullet Hell Infinite is an "infite runner" game where the player must dodge dozens of projectiles and enemies to survive.

While playing different power-ups can be collected to empower to player and change how their ship behaves:

  • Double Shot
  • Triple Shot
  • Super Speed
  • Invincibility
  • Healing
At the end of a game the player's score is saved to a file and the top scores can be viewed from the menu.
